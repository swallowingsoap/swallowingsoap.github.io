About The Film

Swallowing Soap combines social satire and body horror to create a vibrant and absurdist world favouring self-censorship over self-expression. On the night of Richard’s house party, his best friend Spencer assures him his newfound sanitised speech will help him fit in, while Richard struggles and eventually meets his demise at the fault of his foul language.
